Sam Griner - Age, Family, Bio

Publish date: 2024-02-13

Internet celebrity who, as a toddler, became the source of the popular internet meme known as "Success Kid." The meme depicts him clenching his fist in what many internet users have interpreted as a moment of triumph. 

Before Fame

He was eleven months old when the "Success Kid" photo was taken at a local park bench.

Trivia

His father, Justin Griner, underwent a successful kidney transplant in 2015 after setting up a GoFundMe campaign which raised more than $100,000. 

Family Life

His mother, Laney Griner, took the now-famous photo of him.

Associated With

He and Kyle Craven both became well-known memes as children.
