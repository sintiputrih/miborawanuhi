Mahesh Babu's Mother Indira Devi Passes Away

Publish date: 2024-05-24

Article by Suman M Published on: 3:48 am, 28 September 2022

Mahesh Babu’s mother Indira Devi breathed her last in the early hours today (Wednesday).

Indira Devi had been admitted to AIG hospitals a few days back due to ill health. Her health was critical and was being treated on the ventilator.

Recently, Mahesh Babu’s brother Ramesh Babu passed away due to prolonged illness. Mahesh Babu shares a great bond with his mother. Prayers and strength to his family during the grieving time.
