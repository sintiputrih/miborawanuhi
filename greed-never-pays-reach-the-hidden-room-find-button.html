Cyberpunk 2077 Greed Never Pays

Publish date: 2024-05-17

Greed Never Pays Cyberpunk 2077 gig is a side mission that you can get in Japantown, and the first bit requires you to search Leah Gladen’s apartment, looking for a button that opens a hidden room. It can easily be a point where you stumble, because it is a bit of a puzzle in an otherwise straightforward quest. It’s not the most difficult of conundrums, but still. If you’re stuck at this point, then our Cyberpunk 2077 Greed Never Pays – Reach the Hidden Room, Find Button guide is the right place to be.

How to Reach Hidden Room in Cyberpunk 2077 Greed Never Pays – Where to Find Button in Leah Gladen’s Apartment

To reach the hidden room in Leah Gladen’s apartment in the Cyberpunk 2077 Greed Never Pays gig, you have to find the secret button. Well, it’s not that secret; it’s pretty obvious, if I’m being honest. All you have to do is scan the kitchen, to the left of the front door. You’ll find the button beneath the kitchen counter.

After you push the button, head to the area to the right of the front door. The secret room is immediately to your right. Access the computer and read the two important messages. This will take you to the next step – going to the braindance club Wired Head.

After that, the quest is relatively straightforward. Go to Wired Head and sneak around or take everyone out. Search everyone, and you’ll find what you’re after. If you pay a visit to the bathroom, you’ll even see what happened to Leah. Drop your cargo at the designated Drop Point, and you’re done.

How to Go to Leah Gladen’s Apartment in Greed Never Pays Cyberpunk 2077 Gig?

To go to Leah Gladen’s Apartment in the Greed Never Pays gig in Cyberpunk 2077, there are several things that you can do. For example, you can use quickhack on the elevator terminal to open the door and head up. You can steal the access token from the receptionist by causing a distraction (or just take everyone down non-lethally, like we did, and take token). You can also take the stairs and go through the maintenance door, if you have a high enough Tech Ability. Last, but not least, you can clamber up the scaffolding and enter the apartment via open window. The code to the apartment door is 2173, but the game will outright tell you that.
