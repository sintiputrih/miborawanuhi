WWE NXT Results (12/5/23)

Publish date: 2024-09-11

Last Chance Iron Survivor Qualifying Fatal 4-Way Match: Roxanne Perez vs. Fallon Henley vs. Thea Hail w/Jacy Jayne vs. Kiana James

As soon as the bell rings, Perez and James attack each other. Hail and Henley send James out of the ring. The Women’s Champion, Lyra Valkyria, is shown watching the match backstage. Tatum Paxley is watching Valkyria. Perez, Henley, and Hail trade pinning combination. Perez goes up top, but James pushes her off. Hail sends Henley out of the ring. Before she can dive, she’s cut off by James. James gets tripped into the bottom rope. Henley decks James.

James pulls Perez in the way of a diving Hail. James grabs her loaded purse and hits Perez with it. Before Perez can react, James launches her into the barricade. After the break, Perez and James fight on the barricade. James and Perez fall off the barricade through the commentary desk. Back in the ring, Hail tries to roll up Henley. Henley counters and hits a running knee for the win.

Winner- Fallon Henley

Backstage, Lola Vice and Elektra Lopez interrupt Lyra Valkyria’s interview. Vice threatens to cash in her championship contract before NXT Deadline. Tatum Paxley walks in and threatens Lopez. Valkyria looks confused.

Backstage, Wes Lee is talking to one of the production staff. Lee grabs a crutch and starts to make his way to the ring.

In-Ring Segment: Wes Lee

Lee says he imagined smacking Dirty Dom all around the arena at NXT Deadline, but unfortunately, that’s not going to happen. Lee says the fan’s support won’t eliminate the pain he feels in his leg just by standing here. He’s going to need surgery, but he promises he will be back stronger than ever. For now, he’ll see us later. Dominik Mysterio’s music hits. Dominik walks out on the ramp and makes fun of Lee for being injured. The crowd boos.

Dominik says he is taking the night off at NXT Deadline. Lee says he won’t be facing him, but Dominik will still have a match. Rey Mysterio appears on the ‘Tron and announces Dominik will meet Dragon Lee at Deadline. Mysterio will be there to watch the match live. Dragon Lee walks to the ring. Dominik tries a sneak attack. Dragon Lee almost hits a powerbomb. Dominik runs away.

In the trainer’s room, Perez and James get into another brawl.

Lola Vice w/Elektra Lopez vs. Tatum Paxley

Paxley does a roll to avoid Vice. Instead of popping to her feet, Paxley does a Daffney-esq pose. Paxley slams Vice. Vice tries to crawl away. Paxley chokes Vice using the bottom rope. Vice traps Paxley in a triangle. Paxley breaks it up with a powerbomb. Vice kicks out. Paxley eventually walks into Vice’s Black Mass. Vice pins Paxley.

Winner- Lola Vice

Six-Person Tag Match: The Alpha Academy vs. Meta-Four

Gable and Co. take an early advantage. The ring gets cleared, and the only two people left are Otis and Lash Legend. Otis gyrates at Legend. Legend bodyslams Otis! Dupri dives off the top and hits a rana on Legend. Legend gets knocked out of the ring, and Otis catches her. Dar tries the Nova Roller, but Gable reverses it into the ankle lock. Dar taps out.

Winners- The Alpha Academy

Women’s Iron Survivor Summit

Byron Saxton is hosting this summit. Tiffany Stratton, Lash Legend, Blair Davenport, Kelani Jordan, and Fallon Henley are in the ring. Each woman has agreed to let the other speak and can only interrupt if their name is mentioned. Stratton says she’s the favorite and she is special because she got a shootout from Charlotte Flair. Jordan says she has no idea what to expect in this match, but she’s going to win. Lash Legend runs down everyone else in the ring. The segment devolves into everyone bickering and Saxton losing it because they promised they wouldn’t. Henley slaps Stratton, and a huge brawl breaks out.

Axiom vs. Nathan Frazer

Axiom and Frazer brawl. All of the women from the Iron Survivor Challenge Summit are still fighting. The fight spills into the ring, causing a disqualification.

No Contest

Nikkita Lyons appears and almost kicks Blair Davenport’s head off.

Last Chance Iron Survivor Qualifying Fatal 4-Way Match: Carmelo Hayes vs. Tyler Bate vs. Joe Coffey vs. Eddy Thorpe

Coffey is dumped over the top. Hayes, Thorpe, and Bate brawl. Bate lands a diving European uppercut, followed by airplane spins for Thorpe and Coffey. Bate sends Hayes flying with a dropkick. Coffey sends Bate into the ropes. Bate eats a spinning facebuster by Coffey. Hayes surprises Thorpe with a clothesline.

Bate takes out Coffey, who gets blasted by Hayes, who walks into a spinebuster by Thorpe. After the break, Coffey spinebusters Thorpe on the ring steps. The referee calls for the medical staff to check on Thorpe. Hayes hits the first 48 on Bate. Hayes tries a springboard, but Coffey catches him with the Glasgow Sendoff. Bate hits a rebound lariat, followed by the Tyler Driver on Coffey for the win.

Winner- Tyler Bate

After the match, Bate says he’s going to war at Deadline. Dijak interrupts. Before he finishes his thought, he tells Bate to hold on. Dijak takes a moment to kick Eddy Thorpe while he’s down. Bron Breakker walks down to the ring. Breakker promises to send them all to the penalty box. His advice to them is to stay in the box. Briggs is out next. Briggs says he knows they are underestimating him. He’s betting on himself. Briggs promises to shock the world at Deadline. Trick Williams walks to the ring next. Williams says he’s not in the mood, so he’s going to whoop each one of them. Dijak tells Williams the only trick he should be whooping is his so-called friend. Williams decks Dijak. Everyone brawls.

Face-to-Face

Both Baron Corbin and Ilja Dragunov are in the ring. Corbin says he’ll go first since he is the challenger. Dragunov says Corbin will not. Dragunov tosses the chair he was sitting in. Dragunov says he can deal with Corbin, but he won’t deal with Corbin, making light of the sacrifices he’s made to be here. Corbin makes fun of the fact that Dragunov left his son when he could have brought his whole family with him. Corbin says Dragunov is just making excuses. Dragunov gets hot after Corbin says he left his son just like his father left him.

Dragunov tells Corbin he’s trying not to lose it because if he does, Corbin will not make it to Deadline. Corbin tries to goad Dragunov into a fight. Corbin sets up a table in the corner and turns his back. He tells Dragunov to do it for his family. Dragunov turns Corbin around and locks his hands around him like he’s going to hit a head and arm suplex.

Dragunov tells Corbin the only one who can beat the dragon is the dragon. Corbin looks concerned. Dragunov laughs. The Men’s Iron Survivor competitors fight out onto the stage and end up in the ring. Breakker tries to Spear Dijak but ends up Spearing Williams through a table. Bate floors Dijak with a rebound lariat.
