Andrew D. Gillum (born July 26, 1979) is an American Politician and Mayor of Tallahassee from Tallahassee, Florida. He is the youngest person who ever elected for Tallahassee City Commission at the age of 23. Furthermore, on August 28, 2018, he became the Florida Democratic Party’s nominee for Governor of Florida.

In fact, the 39-year-old politician won in Florida’s Democratic primary for governor. Gillum became the state’s first African-American gubernatorial nominee in Florida. Moreover, the United States does not have a single African American governor ever.
