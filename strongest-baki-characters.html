The 10 Strongest Baki Characters: Who Would Win?

Publish date: 2024-04-22

In the impatient world of Baki, where the pursuit of strength knows no bounds, ten iconic characters have risen above all others, etching their names as legends of combat. These herculean warriors have shattered bones and crushed skulls, pushing the limits of sanity in their quest to be recognized as the mightiest humans on Earth. Each one possesses awe-inspiring abilities that defy imagination. Let us delve into the pantheon of the strongest Baki characters of all time.

Strongest Characters of Baki 

  • Yujiro Hanma – The Unrivaled Monster

    • Standing atop the pinnacle of strength, Yujiro Hanma is hailed as the ‘strongest on earth.’ His power surpasses that of entire nations’ modern militaries, and he remains undefeated. His devastating strikes create craters in concrete walls, and his blinding speed renders him imperceptible. Yujiro’s unparalleled combat prowess is only rivaled by his unrivaled knowledge of human anatomy, though he relishes prolonging the thrill of battle before vanquishing his foes.

  • Musashi Miyamoto – The Reincarnated Samurai

    • The legendary samurai, Musashi Miyamoto, finds new life in the modern era through voodoo magic and science. His legendary feats include single-handedly defeating an entire army at just seventeen years old. Armed with the Niten Ichi-Ryu, Musashi wields swords with lethal precision, though his hands are equally lethal, cleaving through multiple opponents with ease.

  • Baki Hanma – The Tenacious Prodigy

    • The central protagonist of the series, Baki Hanma’s journey began with a single goal: to surpass his father, Yujiro. Intense training and clashes with exceptional fighters refined his mixed martial arts prowess, blending karate, pro boxing, Kempo, and jujutsu into a formidable fighting style. During his fateful battle with Yujiro, Baki demonstrated the power to harm his seemingly invincible father.

  • Biscuit Oliva – The Muscular Marvel

    • Renowned as the most muscular man in the U.S., Biscuit Oliva’s strength is as awe-inspiring as his physique. Repelling shotgun blasts with ease, Oliva’s muscles are dense and resilient. Pulling helicopters with bare hands maintains his impressive strength, while his remarkable recovery is fueled by his unconventional remedy of healing injuries with steak.

  • Pickle – The Prehistoric Behemoth

    • Preserved for 200 million years, Pickle hails from the Cretaceous Era, where he battled and consumed even T-Rexes with his monstrous strength. Bullets are but mere annoyances to his impenetrable muscles. Having lived amidst untamed nature, Pickle’s inhuman endurance, durability, and strength surpass modern comprehension.

  • Kaoru Hanayama – The Invincible Yakuza

    • The strongest Yakuza in Japan, Kaoru Hanayama wields monstrous physical strength as his weapon of choice. His grip is deadly, capable of constricting blood flow and causing opponents’ limbs to explode. Through rigorous training, Hanayama developed an insusceptible body, impervious to pain, and his nearly indestructible form can endure the most devastating attacks.

  • Nomi no Sukune – The Unyielding Titan

    • Sukune’s grip strength is nothing short of unbelievable, surpassing even that of his formidable ancestors. His vice-like grip can crush coal into diamonds, exerting pressure equivalent to 100 tons of atmosphere. Despite his colossal size, Sukune exhibits remarkable agility, causing mini earthquakes with his stomps and delivering powerful kicks with astonishing flexibility.

  • Kaku Kaiou – The Timeless Master

    • At an astonishing 146 years old, Kaku Kaiou reigns as the strongest of all Kaious in Baki’s realm. A supreme grandmaster of Chinese Kempo, he defied age by sending even the invincible Yujiro Hanma flying. Kaku’s swift and precise hand chops can sever limbs effortlessly, a testament to his mastery over strength and technique.

  • Jack Hanma – The Tenacious Challenger

    • As the brother of Baki and son of the legendary Yujiro Hanma, Jack’s obsession to defeat his father drove him to relentless training. The consumption of the unique X-4 drug granted him rapid muscle growth, augmenting his already prodigious strength. Beyond sheer force, Jack possesses tactical intelligence, strategically outmaneuvering muscle-bound adversaries.

  • Kaiou Retsu – The Peerless Martial Artist

    • Bestowed with the esteemed title of “Kaiou” for his unparalleled mastery of Chinese martial arts, Retsu quickly proved himself as the greatest in 4000 years. A true polymath, he wields deadly weapons with ease, making the nine-section whips, Dao, and throwing knives mere extensions of his formidable prowess. With his bare fists, Retsu can pulverize boulders into perfect spheres, effortlessly sending grown men soaring meters into the air.

    The world of Baki is a crucible of unfathomable strength and skill, where these ten iconic characters etch their names as the mightiest humans to have ever graced the Earth. Their indomitable spirits, unparalleled abilities, and unyielding determination make them the stuff of legends in the world of combat.
