11 Ways to Wear a Leggings Outfit for Any Occasion

Publish date: 2024-04-13

There's a reason a great pair of leggings is counted among almost just about everyone's wardrobe staples. Leggings are notoriously comfy AF, but they can also be dressed up to a surprisingly high-fashion degree. From street style stars and celebs to the runways of New York and red carpets of Los Angeles, you'll find that leggings have been making appearances in all forms — casual or dressy.

Leggings have emerged as a great piece to pull on when you want to rock a barely there trend (think: crochet, fishnet, distressed, sheer) without baring everything. Whether gym-ready or dry-clean-only, the options are endless and versatile. While we all adore our basic black pair, there's a world of patterns and materials to explore to keep the comfort flowing while elevating the look, from leather to silk and even patterned and monogrammed options.

To give you a little inspiration, here are our favorite leggings outfit ideas.

Layer Under Crochet

If extra coverage affords you lots of extra comfort, we definitely recommend wearing a pair of cotton leggings under a crochet skirt. Pro tip: Finding a perfectly matching pair of leggings will take this look from "on trend" to "iconic."

Mix Stripes and Sheer

Stripes and sheer are great on their own, but when they come together, the result is truly *chef's kiss.* We recommend letting the boldness of your leggings dictate the rest of your ensemble's color palette, but you can also take the guesswork out by sticking to neutral

Pair with a Sporty Windbreaker

Put a little pep into the step of an athleisure look by pairing colorfully striped crew socks, white sneakers, and classic black leggings with a sports bra and colorful windbreaker for a casual look that's a little bit '90s and a whole lot fresh.

Wear Under a Sheer Dress

Not sure what to wear under a sheer dress? Why, you could layer one over a pair of textured leggings, of course. A shiny vinyl or faux leather look adds an air of intentionally so you'll look confident rather than covered up.

Coordinate with a Plunging Bodysuit

Slipping on something comfortable is a great way to gas yourself up for trying a look that might be a little bit more daring than you're used to. Case in point: a plunging bodysuit or top that requires more than a few pieces of fashion tape. It's definitely one of the edgiest looks on our list, but trust us when we say the leggings pull it all together, as Bebe Rexha demonstrates.

Pick a Print or Monochromatic Look

We love a look that's as bold as it is comfortable. Create a showstopping moment by putting together basics that share the same print or color for an intentional 'fit that's low-key comfy.

Wear Under an Oversized Tee

A satin bomber jacket thrown over an oversized T-shirt atop basic black leggings is an easy go-to and perfectly embodies street-style casual vibes.

Go Asymmetrical

Now for another night-out approved leggings look that's perfect for warmer weather. Start with a strappy, asymmetrical crop top or bodysuit, add a belt at the waist, and pair it with a metallic bag and strappy sandals.

Add a Vest

Vests are back in a major way, and there's no sleeker styling option than rocking one with a pair of shiny black leggings. Elevate the elongating silhouette by adding a pair of heeled boots.

Accent with Statement Boots

Take advantage of the body-hugging silhouette of leggings and make a pair of chunky platform boots the star. This works particularly well with a leather pair of leggings but isn't a requirement for instantly upping your leggings's cool factor.

Throw on a Blazer

Bring a little business to your fave casual leggings look by adding an oversized blazer over the top. A monochromatic or black-and-white color palette keeps things chic, while a quietly luxurious bag completes the look.
