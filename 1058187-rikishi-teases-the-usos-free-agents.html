Rikishi Celebrates The Usos Winning WWE Gold Again, Teases Their Contract Status

Publish date: 2024-08-21

The Usos won their sixth WWE Tag Team championship at Elimination Chamber, but that doesn’t mean their long-term status within WWE is certain. It was previously reported that the duo’s current contract would be up in April and now their father, Rikishi, is teasing that they’ll be free agents soon.

On Instagram, Rikishi celebrated their win by saying that their hard work “will never be denied in the WWE or anywhere else in the wrestling world.” He went on to use the hashtag #TimesUp and #SoonToBeFreeAgents before ending with a cryptic “or not.”
