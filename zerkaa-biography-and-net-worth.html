Zerkaa Biography And Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-04-09

Zerkaa is a famous English Video gaming enthusiast who is best known for posting sports video game FIFA in his videos.

Zerkaa was born Josh Zerker on September 4, 1992, in England. Not much information about his parents and siblings is available to the media.

Zerkaa studied business in high school but later went to the University to major in film making.

Zerkaa joined Youtube in 2009 and created his own channel. He began posting videos with the game Call of Duty: Modern Warfare II. He has a second channel ZerkaaPlays where he posted videos of him playing other different games that are trending in the market.

Zerkaa commands over 4 million subscribers and posted close to 2,000 videos so far on his channel.

Zerkaa net worth

Zerkaa is estimated to have a net worth of $6 million. Youtube is his major income source plus other side gigs.
