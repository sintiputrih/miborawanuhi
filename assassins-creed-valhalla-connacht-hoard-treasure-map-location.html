Connacht Hoard Map Treasure Location

Publish date: 2024-05-05

Connacht Hoard Map is one of the Artifacts you can find when you reach the third region in Ireland (Connacht) as part of the Assassin’s Creed Valhalla Wrath of the Druids DLC. It is the third and final Hoard Treasure in Connacht, following the Dream Of Druids and To My Friend, The Priest.

Furthermore, the Wrath Of Druids Connacht Hoard Map is, unlike the previous two we have covered, an actual map, but it’s a bit challenging because of the drawing on it. So, assuming you need help, here is how to get the Connacht Hoard Treasure.

Where To Find the AC Valhalla Connacht Hoard Map

Let’s start with the map’s location because you can’t find the treasure without it. The Connacht Treasure Map is located on the solitary island known as Downpatrick Head. This island is found on the western side of Connacht, northwest of Port Auley.

When you get to the location on the map above, you’ll want to find a hole in the ground blocked by a breakable floor. It’s on the northwestern side of the island and requires an explosive arrow, mainly because there are no explosive jars nearby. Granted, you can carry one from Port Auley using a boat but using an explosive arrow, it’s faster. So, shoot an explosive arrow to destroy the floor pictured below, and then inside the hole, you’ll find the Connacht Treasure Map.

Awesome job! After you get the map, let’s see what it shows. Open it by accessing Eivor’s inventory, and you’ll be able to see a drawing. On it, you will see:

  • A medium island surrounded by rivers
  • There is also a river crossing the southern side
  • North of the river is a well inside a hole
  • The treasure is inside the well

Where To Find the Connacht Hoard Treasure

The island where you will find the Connacht Hoard Treasure is southeast of your current location. Specifically, it is east of Lough Gara, in the very center of Connacht. If you zoom in on your map, you’ll see the name of the Ballysadare River east of it, just like in my screenshot below.

Travel to this small island, and you’ll find a deep canyon. Go all the way to the bottom and look for a well with a wooden cover (also pictured).

Destroy the cover, then get inside, and when you reach the bottom, you’ll find the glowing treasure spot you can loot. In the first DLC of the video game developed by Ubisoft, the Connacht Hoard Treasure unlocks the Na Pucai Scheme (Sail).
