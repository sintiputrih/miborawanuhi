Jason Kennedy Biography

Robert Jason Kennedy, popularly known as Jason Kennedy, is an acclaimed American Entertainment Journalist and the host of E! News, at E! Networks. Jason Kennedy Career

Jason Kennedy E! News

Jason started his career at E! News in 2005. From the time when he joined the E! Networks team, he has hosted the Daily 10 show. He has been a weekend anchor for E! News, and has hosted the “Last Bride Standing” special for E!.

In early 2007, Jason covered the death of Anna Nicole Smith near his South Florida hometown. As of May 2008, he hosted his first Primetime show named Dance Machine on ABC.

Jason was named one of People Magazine’s “Hottest Bachelors of 2008”. He was also named one of ten young stars who will become a leader of the Broadcast industry, in the Los Angeles Times article “Clark Trek: Who Will Become The Next Dick Clark?”
