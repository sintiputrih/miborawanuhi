Publish date: 2024-02-24

Update, 8/6: The Club Monaco dress that Meghan Markle wore to the Van Straubenzee wedding has now sold out.

Original story, 8/4: August 4, 2018 marks two very special occasions for Prince Harry. Not only is it his wife's birthday, but it also happens to be the wedding day of his close friend, Charlie van Straubenzee.

Instead of celebrating Meghan Markle's birthday, Prince Harry has been enlisted as van Straubenzee's best man. Having such an important role in the wedding means that both the Duke and Duchess of Sussex are attending the wedding in the village of Churt in Surrey this weekend.

After their PDA-filled appearance at the Sentebale polo match in Windsor last week, Prince Harry's Nacho Figueras opened up about the newlyweds.
