Jonathan Bullard is an American sportsman. He is an American football defensive end for the Atlanta Falcons of the National Football League (NFL).

Bullard was born on October 22, 1993, in Shelby, North Carolina. He attended Crest Senior High School in Shelby, North Carolina.

Bullard was ranked as the best defensive end in his class. He then furthered his education at the University of Florida.

While in Florida, Bullard played college football. He was drafted by the Chicago Bears in the third round of the 2016 NFL Draft.

Bullard formerly played for the Arizona Cardinals and Seattle Seahawks.
