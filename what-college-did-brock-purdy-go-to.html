What college did Brock Purdy go to?

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Brock Purdy, the talented American football quarterback, attended Iowa State University, where he made a significant impact on the college football scene. Here's a detailed look at his college career:

Early Years and High School Achievements

Brock Purdy was born on December 27, 1999, in Queen Creek, Arizona, and played high school football for Perry High School in Gilbert, Arizona. During his tenure at Perry, Purdy achieved a 27–13 record and was named Gatorade's Football Player of the Year and The Arizona Republic's high school football player of the year.

College Career at Iowa State University

After finishing his high school career, Purdy committed to Iowa State University, where he played as a quarterback from 2018 to 2021. At Iowa State, he quickly rose to prominence, gaining national attention as a sophomore and eventually becoming one of the greatest quarterbacks in Cyclone history.
