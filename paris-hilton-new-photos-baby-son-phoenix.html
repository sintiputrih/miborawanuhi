Paris Hilton Marks 6 Months of Motherhood With New Photos of Baby Son Phoenix

Publish date: 2024-03-29

It’s officially been six months since Paris Hilton and husband Carter Reum welcomed their first child.

The heiress celebrated the major milestone with a sweet new Instagram tribute, in which she shared photos of herself and son Phoenix cuddling with each other on the floor of Hilton’s massive foyer. In the pictures, she wears a baby-pink tracksuit with hearts printed down the sides of the pants, plus a pair of blue socks with a white cloud print. Meanwhile, little Phoenix looks adorable in a light blue onesie while carrying a tiny stuffed rabbit.

In a video also included in the post, Hilton carries Phoenix as he interacts with multiple animal toys arranged on a console table.

“This Barbie is a Mom. 💕👱🏼‍♀️👶🏼💕,” Hilton captioned the photos, referencing the marketing campaign for Greta Gerwig’s forthcoming Barbie film. “My baby boy Phoenix is 6 months old today.🥰 He is my world and makes my life feel so complete.🥹 Can’t wait for all the magical memories we will create together!💫 Mommy loves you so much angel baby😇.”

Phoenix was born via surrogacy this past January.

Hilton talked about wanting to protect her son’s privacy in an interview for Harper’s Bazaar’s March 2023 cover story. “My entire life has been so public. I’ve never had anything for myself. We decided that we wanted to have this whole experience to ourselves,” she said. “I want to protect him and to be with him every second. You have this mother instinct that kicks in, which I’ve never had before. I feel so complete now.”
