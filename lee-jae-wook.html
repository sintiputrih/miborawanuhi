How did Lee Jae-Wook start his Professional Career?

Lee Jaewook is a South Korean actor in his twenties. Despite his inexperience, Jae-Wook is widely regarded as one of the best upcoming Korean actors. Furthermore, despite portraying several young characters, many people believe Jae-Wook is much older than he is. He’s also played characters who are much older than he is, as well as characters who are much younger.

He made his acting debut as a hacker-programmer in the drama Memories of the Alhambra. Memories of the Alhambra is a 2018 South Korean drama starring Hyun Bin and Park Shin-Hye.

Jaewook appeared in this drama as Marco Han, a programmer and hacker affiliated with Sejoo, played by Park Chanyeol of the Korean boy band Exo.
Soon after his appearance in Memories of Alhambra, Lee Jaewook was signed to Hyun Bin’s agency VAST Entertainment. From 2018 to 2019, he worked on this k-drama.

Later that year, Jaewook starred in The Battle of Jangsari, also known as Jangsari: Forgotten Heroes. In this film, Jaewook played a student soldier who was sent to Jangsari without proper training for a battle. Popular actors Kim Myung-min, Megan Fox, and Choi Min-ho also play lead roles in this film.

Lee Jaewook has only worked on The Battle of Jangsari as of November 2020. Following that, the young actor starred as Seol Ji-hwan in the 2019 k-drama Search: WWW.In the same year, he starred in Extraordinary You as Baek Kyung. In Search: WWW, he plays one of the main characters, whereas, in Extraordinary You, he plays the female lead’s childhood friend-fiancé.

In the 2020 k-drama When the Weather Is Fine, Lee Jae-Wook played Lee Jang-woo. He worked on this drama with Seo Kang Joon and Park Young. Lee Jang-woo, his character, is a high school friend of the main characters Eun-Seob and Mok Hae-won.

In 2020, Lee Jae-Wook landed his first leading role in a drama. He played Sunwoo Joon in Do Do Sol Sol La La Sol. As the male lead, he co-stars with Go Ara.

Lee Jae-Awards works and Nominations

In his relatively short acting career, Lee Jae Wook, a young rookie actor, has received a few nominations. He has also apprehended some of them.
