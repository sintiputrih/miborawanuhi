Keri Russell News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-16

Name:Keri Russell

Hometown:Fountain Valley, CA

Birthday:March 23, 1976

About

Best known as Felicity Porter from the WB hit TV series Felicity, Keri Russell began acting on the Mickey Mouse Club in 1991 alongside future stars Justin Timberlake, JC Chasez, Britney Spears and Ryan Gosling.

Born in 1976, the California native also landed gigs in 2002’s We Were Soldiers and 2005’s The Upside of Anger. She also appeared in 2007’s indie flick Waitress.

Russell was linked to actor Scott Speedman, but has been married to Shane Deary since 2007. The couple, who share two children, announced their separation in 2013.
