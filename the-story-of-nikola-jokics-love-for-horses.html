The Story of Nikola Jokic's Love for Horses

Publish date: 2024-01-22

NBA Champion and former MVP Nikola Jokic is not just a basketball superstar; he is also a passionate lover of horses.

His connection with these majestic animals runs deep and has been an integral part of his life for many years. Let's delve into the fascinating and heartwarming story of Nikola Jokic's love for horses.

Early Passion for Horses

Joker's love affair with horses began at a young age in his hometown of Sombor, Serbia. His initial exposure to these magnificent creatures occurred when his father took him to a horse race in their city.

It was during this visit that Jokic's fascination with horses ignited, leading to a deep-rooted passion that would stay with him throughout his life.

Bond with Dream Catcher

In 2016, after his first season in the NBA, Joker fulfilled a long-cherished dream by purchasing his first racehorse, Dream Catcher. This significant step marked the beginning of a profound connection with the world of horse racing. Jokic's dedication to training and caring for Dream Catcher became a cherished pastime, providing him with a sense of tranquility and fulfillment outside the realm of basketball.

Strong Ties to Serbia

Jokic's love for horses is intimately woven into the fabric of his life in Serbia. During the offseason, he spends a considerable amount of time at the stables, where his godfather assists in the care of his horses.

His days are often filled with the simple joys of washing, walking, and even riding behind his beloved animals. This deep bond with horses has become an integral part of his life, offering him a sense of peace and contentment.

Uniting Passion and Friendship

The former MVP's passion for horses not only brings him personal joy but has also forged meaningful relationships. His former teammate, now working in the horse racing industry, has become a close friend to Jokic.

Their shared love for horses has deepened their bond, and Jokic often engages in insightful discussions with his friend about various aspects of the craft.

Jokic and the Racing Community

Despite his towering presence as a professional basketball player, Joker has seamlessly integrated into the racing community.

His interactions with trainers, drivers, and fellow enthusiasts reflect his genuine interest in harness racing. Jokic's eagerness to learn from seasoned professionals in the racing world speaks volumes about his dedication to understanding and appreciating the art of horse racing.

Unique Friendship with Tim Tetrick

One of the most captivating aspects of Jokic's love for horses is his unique friendship with Tim Tetrick, a legendary figure in harness racing.

Their friendship transcends professional boundaries, as Jokic's genuine admiration for Tetrick's expertise in the field has blossomed into a strong and enduring bond.

Despite their differing professional domains, their shared passion for horses has united them in a remarkable friendship.

Jokic's Enthusiasm for Racing

The excitement and enthusiasm that Jokic exudes when discussing horses and racing are unmistakable.

His unwavering dedication to this passion is evident in his frequent visits to stables and race tracks, both in Serbia and the United States.

Even amidst the demands of his professional basketball career, Jokic's heart remains intertwined with the world of horse racing, a place where he finds solace and joy.

Impact on Jokic's Personal Life

Jokic's love for horses extends beyond mere appreciation for the sport; it has profoundly impacted his personal life. His teammates have witnessed the depth of his affection for horses, recognizing the calming effect it has on him. Jokic's connection with these animals goes beyond mere admiration; it is a source of tranquility and fulfillment that complements his life as a professional athlete.

The Allure of Horse Racing

For Joker, horse racing is not just a sport; it is a world of beauty, athleticism, and connection. His genuine love for these magnificent animals is reflected in his reverence for their unique characteristics and athletic prowess.

The allure of horse racing has captivated Jokic, drawing him into a world where he finds respite and a deep sense of connection with nature.

Nikola Jokic's love for horses transcends the boundaries of his professional life as a basketball superstar. His enduring passion for horse racing serves as a source of joy, tranquility, and meaningful connections.

 In the world of horse racing, Jokic has found not just a hobby, but a place where his heart and soul find true contentment.
