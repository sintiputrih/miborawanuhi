Are you hoping to sweep your date off their feet with the best cologne for men? We get it. You want to smell fantastic in an effortless, laid-back kind of way. You want to be the guy who can smell incredible after just one spritz.

That’s where we come in. We’re here to help you find the best men’s cologne of all time. We tested the best-selling men’s fragrances to help you smell your best.

    1. Blu Atlas Bountiful Eau De Parfum

    Bountiful Eau de Parfum is the winner of the best men’s cologne of all time. It’s a versatile fragrance that can take you from the office to your best friend’s bachelor party to a sunset walk on a beach in Ibiza. Bountiful Eau de Parfum embodies the steadfast confidence of a man who recognizes the boundless possibilities of the life he lives.

    While versatility is the name of the game for this fragrance, it carries bold, classic notes that give it a warm, grounded scent. This fragrance adds a layer of sophistication to your style. Charm everyone around you with a fragrance that works well in all four seasons and leaves a lasting impression.

    Upon first spritzing the EDP, top notes of lemon, pink peppercorn, and bergamot make a bright entrance. Middle notes of violet, orris, oak moss, cardamom, and hyacinth mellow out the fragrance, driving it into a more woody aroma. Base notes turn up the heat and transform the fragrance into a classic masculine scent with vetiver, cedar, patchouli, musk, and cedarwood.

    As the best men’s cologne of all time, we recommend Bountiful EDP to men looking for a clean, classic scent.

    2. Dior Sauvage Eua de Parfum

    Bold, spicy, fresh, and undeniably delightful-smelling, Dior Sauvage Eau de Parfum has been enchanting men and women since 2015. As an instant hit, the fragrance rose to the top of the charts due to its ability to capture the aroma of a modern man with classic fragrance notes.

    The spicy blend combines woody notes with peppery freshness to give men the cool, modern scent they desire. Dior Sauvage EDP is one of the best-selling fragrances for men because of its sexy, rugged scent, which no one can resist.

    3. Armani Code Eau de Toilette

    Make a statement with Armani Code’s EDT. Made for contemporary men who desire a charming, seductive scent; the EDT is a blend of sultry notes that appeal to men and women. Cedarwood, mandarin, tonka bean, and lavandin are the stars of the show and transform the fragrance into a seductive masterpiece.

    Armani Code EDT is the best men’s cologne for guys who want a tempting fragrance that excites every potential love interest.

    4. Chanel Bleu de Chanel Eau de Parfum

    Take charge with a classic men’s cologne that brings together bold citrus scents with timeless woody aromas. Fresh citrus accord mixes with ambery cedar to give you a fresh, no-nonsense fragrance. Tonka bean, sandalwood, and vanilla give the fragrance a more masculine aroma, ideal for men who want a modern classic.

    Spray the aroma on your wrists first thing in the morning, and carry this rich, bold scent throughout the day. We recommend using another spritz before heading out to nighttime activities to get the most from the fragrance. Chanel Bleu de Chanel EDP is the best cologne for men who want a sophisticated scent.

    5. Paco Rabanne 1 Million Eau de Toilette

    Looking for an undeniably masculine cologne that’s the ultimate weapon for after-hours fun? 1 Million EDT is a fresh, sexy fragrance that’s the perfect sidekick when you’re ready to hit the bars after work or go on a first date.

    1 Million EDT is a blend of spicy, rugged aromas like patchouli, leather, pink cinnamon, and peppermint that creates an ambiance of confidence. The fragrance is one of the brand’s best-selling fragrances and one of the best men’s colognes of all time.

    6. Tom Ford Oud Wood Eau de Parfum

    Men searching for an exotic, smoky blend should look no further. Oud Wood is a popular fragrance that appeals to men who want to smell like the old days. Fires burning under the light of a full moon or riding horseback through the West; the fragrance evokes the feeling of a man who is utterly free.

    Key notes of Chinese pepper, oud wood, and sandalwood make the fragrance a masculine, outdoorsy aroma and transport you to a different time.

    7. Versace Dylan Blu Eau de Toilette

    Dylan Blu from Versace is your perfect match if you dream of long walks through ripe orchards followed by a quick dip in the Mediterranean Sea. The fragrance evokes the feeling of a summer spent lounging on the beach and meeting friends for drinks at your favorite bar.

    A whirlwind of notes, such as bergamot, patchouli, frankincense, fig leaves, saffron, and papyrus, makes the fragrance a delightful aroma that keeps you fresh all day.
