Career

Tom Bilyeu’s success was not handed to him on a silver platter. He had to work hard to become one of the wealthiest men in the world today. Tom described himself as lazy and chubby prior to his fame and fortune. His weight loss inspired him to encourage others, including his obese family, to lose weight as well. He pitched the idea of a healthy nutrition business to his coworkers, who enthusiastically supported it. That is how Quest Nutrition was born. In 2010, he co-founded Quest Nutrition with his wife Lisa, Ron Penna, Shannan Pena, and Mike Osborne.

The company is well-known for its protein bars and chips, as well as its Quest protein powder. By 2013, the nutrition company’s revenue had surpassed $42 million. Quest Nutrition was named the world’s second-fastest-growing company by Inc. 500 in 2014. Quest products had expanded to over 70 countries around the world, with over 40,000 distribution outlets. The company thrives on creating products that taste good while still providing the nutritional value that meals require. They have primarily been used for weight management and control.

Quest protein bars were later sold for $1 billion to Simply Good Foods Co. Quest is currently worth more than $345 million per year. Their products are stocked and sold in major retail chains such as Walmart.

Furthermore, in October 2016, Tom and his wife launched Impact Theory, a new business venture. The profitable media company’s goal is to inspire people to reach their full potential. Tom hosts and uploads lengthy interview talk shows for the company. Wyclef Jean, Dr. Drew, and others are among the guests they’ve interviewed.
