Publish date: 2024-05-28

Is Kendall Jenner pregnant? A rumor has surfaced among fans of the popular reality show, The Kardashians, that the 27-year-old model might be expecting.

In a recent trailer of the Hulu reality series, Jenner was asked about her pregnancy by a producer during an interview. This obviously left fans shocked, as this is the first time the supermodel has spoken about the pregnancy.

Jenner is the only Kardashian-Jenner sister who has yet to have a child, and frequently is pressured by her family to consider it.

Jenner neither confirms nor denies any speculations – leaving reality TV junkies on the edge of their seats awaiting the next episode.

Jenner has recently been linked to rapper, Bad Bunny, but a relationship has not yet been confirmed. The two were most recently spotted after the Met Gala, according to Teen Vogue.
