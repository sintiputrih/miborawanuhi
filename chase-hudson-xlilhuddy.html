Chase Hudson is an American social media personality who became popular with his comedic clips on TikTok. He has garnered more than 31.7 million followers on the platform where he entertains in collaboration with his friends and lip-syncs to songs of famed musicians like Justin Bieber and Pitbull. His insane fame on TikTok has led to a strong fan base on other social media as well. He has more than 10.5 million followers on Instagram and more than 2.8 million followers on Twitter. Chase also runs a YouTube channel, which has more than 2 million subscribers, where he vlogs and his family members, especially his mother and sisters, regularly feature. He is good friends with social media star Payton Moormeier and frequently collaborates with him on various platforms.

    Chase Hudson Facts

  • He was a member of the 2019 Lights Out Summer Tour, a countrywide co-headlining concert tour. He featured in the touring party with popular social media personalities such as Payton Moormeier, Anthony Reeves, Noen Eubanks, Jaden Hossler, Sam Hurley, Cynthia Parker, Jackson Felt, Chase Keith, Griffin Johnson, Mychaelade, Kevin Perry, Christopher Romero, Sebastian Topete, Tayson Madkour, Conner Shane, Yewsif, Luigi Castillo, Brooke Blach, and Katelyn McFadden.
  • In 2019, he co-founded the Hype House along with other social media personalities like Alex Warren, Kouvr Annon, and Thomas Petrou.
  • In December 2020, Chase signed an exclusive contract with Interscope Records.
  • Chase has an irrational fear of heights.
  • Chase has a line of personalized merchandise that he sells online through his website. His collection includes T-shirts, beanies, hoodies, hats, joggers, backpacks, phone cases, pillows, and bracelets.
  • Chase has stated that he has ambitions of branching out of his work on social media and starting a career in modeling, although he would like to continue being a YouTuber.
  • Chase made his acting debut in 2021 with Machine Gun Kelly‘s musical film Downfalls High in a lead role. The film also starred Sydney Sweeney, rapper Trippie Redd, Iann Dior, and Blackbear.
  • In January 2021, Chase released his debut song along with a music video titled 21st Century Vampire.
  • On February 18, 2021, Chase released his second song The Eulogy of You and Me along with a music video. Chase revealed that the song was especially for those who spent Valentine’s Day alone.
  • In April 2021, Chase released a new song America’s Sweetheart which also starred Charli D’Amelio (his ex-girlfriend). Charli also inspired him for the song.
  • In August 2021, he released his new single titled Don’t Freak Out.
  • In August 2021, Chase collaborated with Glamnetic to release a new gel nail sticker line.
  • In September 2021, Chase collaborated with Burger King to launch his own limited Burger King meal as a part of their “Keep it Real Meals” campaign. The meal included a Spicy Ch’King with cheese, four-piece mozzarella sticks, and a 16-ounce chocolate shake.
  • In September 2021, Chase released his debut LP Teenage Heartbreak.
  • Chase was included in the 2022 list of Forbes 30 Under 30.
  • In July 2022, he released a new song titled All the Things I Hate About You.
  • In the past, Paper magazine had cited him as an important figure in the 2020s pop-punk revival.
