ESPYs 2017: Complete List of Nominees and Winners

Publish date: 2024-02-07

A celebration of excellence! Top athletes and celebrities gathered on Wednesday, July 12, to commemorate the past year in sports at the 2017 ESPY Awards. Former Indianapolis Colts and Denver Broncos quarterback Peyton Manning hosted the show’s 25th anniversary.

Former first lady Michelle Obama honored Eunice Kennedy Shriver with the Arthur Ashe Courage Award for her role in creating the Special Olympics.
