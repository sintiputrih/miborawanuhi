Queen Silvia of Sweden net worth: Queen Silvia of Sweden is the current wife of King Carl XVI Gustaf and has a net worth of $10 million dollars. Born in Heidelberg, Germany on December 23, 1943, Silvia’s full name is Silvia Renate and is the daughter of Walther Sommerlath and Alice Soares de Toledo. She is a member of the House of Bernadotte as well as the Church of Sweden and became queen consort of Sweden on June 19, 1976. Prior to her marriage to the King of Sweden, Silvia worked as a flight attendant and was a former educational host for the 1972 Summer Olympics. She is also an interpreter with expertise in Swedish, German, Portuguese, French, Spanish, and English. SHe has three children with the King including Crown Princess Victoria, Prince Carl Philip, and Princess Madeleine. She co-founded the World Childhood Foundation in 1999 and is chairman for the Royal Wedding Fund as well as Queen Silvia’s Jubilee Fund.
