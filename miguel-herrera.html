Biography

Miguel Herrera was born in Hidalgo, Mexico on March 18, 1968. Soccer manager best known for coaching teams like Club America in Liga MX and the Mexican national team. He coached Mexico at the 2014 FIFA World Cup. He and Hugo Sanchez are both former soccer players who went on to coach the Mexican national team. He made his debut as a player with Atlante in 1986 before transferring to Santos Laguna two seasons later.
