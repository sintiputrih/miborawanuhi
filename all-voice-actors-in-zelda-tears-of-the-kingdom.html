All Voice Actors in Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom

Publish date: 2024-09-08

The new Legend Tears of the Kingdom has voice acting just like Breath of the Wild, and we’ve got the full cast of voice actors for you here. There are some famous names in the cast, and some fan-favourite characters returning to the spotlight. Keep reading for the full voice cast and their characters, including the voices of Ganondorf, Zelda, Rauru and more.

Tears of the Kingdom: Ganondorf, Zelda, Rauru voice actors

Here’s a list of voice actors for prominent characters in Tears of the Kingdom.

Ganondorf – Matthew Mercer

Iconic voice actor Matthew Mercer is making an appearance in the Legend of Zelda franchise as the villain Ganondorf. This legendary villain is one of the most exciting parts of Tears of the Kingdom‘s story, and he has an amazing voice thanks to Mercer.

Zelda – Patricia Summerset

Patricia Summerset is reprising her role as Princess Zelda in Tears of the Kingdom. She also featured in Breath of the Wild, as well as Hyrule Warriors: Age of Calamity.

Rauru – Chris Hackney

Chris Hackney will voice Rauru in Tears of the Kingdom. This character is returning to the franchise after a long while away, and fans are already talking about his voice.

Tears of the Kingdom: Link voice actor & more

Link – Kengo Takanishi

Interestingly, Link does have a voice actor. He doesn’t speak, but he does a lot of grunting, and that grunting is performed by Kengo Takanishi.

Sidon – James Mortellaro

Prince Sidon is returning in Tears of the Kingdom, and he’s voiced by James Mortellaro. This time around, Link actually gets to fight enemies alongside him.

Tarin – Rob Riddell

The game’s IMDb page doesn’t list many voice actors, but it does list Rob Riddell as Tarin. This character has the same name as the guy who looks like Mario from Link’s Awakening, bizarrely. Will he be returning in Tears of the Kingdom?

Tulin – Cristina Vee

Cristina Vee voices the character Tulin in Tears of the Kingdom. If you played its predecessor, you might remember Tulin as the baby chick you meet in the village near the flying Divine Beast.

Riju – Elizabeth Maxwell

Riju is a lightning-wielding Gerudo in Tears of the Kingdom, and she’s voiced by voice actor Elizabeth Maxwell.
