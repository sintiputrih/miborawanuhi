Frank Marshall was an American film producer and director born on September 13, 1946 in Glendale, CA. He was best known for his work alongside his wife Kathleen Kennedy, producing films such as Raiders of the Lost Ark, Back to the Future, and Jurassic Park. He was also a director, having directed films such as Arachnophobia and Alive.
