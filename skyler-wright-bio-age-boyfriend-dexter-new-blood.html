Meet Skyler Wright! Her Bio, Age, Boyfriend, Family, Dexter: New Blood

Publish date: 2024-09-19

Showtime’s Dexter that ended after eight seasons in 2013, came back for its sequel titled Dexter: New Blood in Nov 2021. And though there were many new characters on the resurrected series, “Chloe” played by Skyler Wright, stood out from the rest, all thanks to her green hair and uncanny performance.

This Skyler Wright Bio explores everything you need to know about this amazing actress. 

Meet Skyler Wright, The Actress Playing Chole In Dexter: New Blood

Skyler Wright’s character “Chloe” first made her appearance in the local diner while talking to Kurt Caldwell (Clancy Brown). After a while,  Kurt then hands her a bus fare to get back to her mom, but she spends it all on food instead.

Seeing this, Kurt then refuses to hand her more money and instead offers her a job. Yet again, Chloe refuses. However, Kurt was able to provide her spare room in his cabin, and that was where the story took a dark turn.

After staying in the cabin for a while Chloe then finds herself restricted from leaving. She screams and hears footsteps above her, and also finds a camera with the words “You’re already dead” written on it.

Do watch the series if you find this plot interesting.

This season, Skyler was joined by new faces like Michael C. Hall (Dexter Morgan / Jim Lindsay), Jack Alcott (Harrison Morgan / Randall), Julia Jones (Angela Bishop), Johnny Sequoyah (Audrey), Alano Miller (Logan), and Jennifer Carpenter (Debra Morgan).

“Beyond excited” for being on the show, Skyler took it to her IG on Nov 3, 2021, to write, “I can’t believe the time has come. Everyone has put so much blood (lol), sweat, and tears into this show over the last year.”

How Old Is Skyler Wright?

Skyler Wright was 24 years of age when she first appeared on Dexter: New Blood in 2021.

Does Skyler Wright Have A Boyfriend?

Skyler Wright didn’t seem like she was dating anyone as of 2021. Also, they were no traces of her romantic life over her socials.

For those of you confused, Tye Sheridan isn’t Skyler’s boyfriend. Well, he was her on-screen boyfriend but the two were only connected professionally outside the screen.

And for anyone who is looking forward to trying their luck with Skyler, you should be prepared to accept that you’re girlfriend is an actress. She doesn’t hesitate to lock lips with others if the role demanded it. Proof? Well, back in 2017, she did share a kiss with Joe Keery for the Domino’s commercial.

Skyler Wright Net Worth

Skyler Wright garnered a net worth of under $450 thousand by 2021.

An actress by profession, Skyler made her debut in the movie industry as “Tricia” in the 2014 movie The Forger. She then went on to appear in over 15 movies and TV shows after that. Naming some of her popular ones are — FML (2016), Sleep Tight (2016), Ghostmates (2016), 9-1-1 (2018), Speechless (2018), A Daughter’s Deception (2019), and NOS4A2 (2019).

Also, Skyler has appeared on theater shows like The Beggars Opera (2015), Proof (2014), The Cagebirds (2013), S.P.A.R, You’re a Good Man, Charlie Brown, Into the Woods, Peter Pan, and many more.

As for her education, Skyler graduated from Duxbury High School and went on to hone her acting, singing, and dancing skills at New York University, Berklee College of Music, Broadway Artists Alliance, UCB, and many other prestigious programs. 

This talented actress was first signed by a Boston Talent Agency and later went on to earn recognition from the International Thespian Society for having completed over 1,000 hours of theatre. Also, she has received numerous acting awards from the M.E.T.G. (Massachusetts Educational Theatre Guild). 

But besides acting, Skyler is also blessed with a talent for singing. Do check her covers over her Youtube and Soundcloud.

As of 2021, she was managed by Ayers Talent Agency (Tim Ayers) in New York, and Archetype Management (Ray Miller) in Los Angeles.
