Where will the Masters take place in 2024?

Publish date: 2024-05-31

The 2024 Masters Tournament, the 88th edition of the prestigious golf championship, is set to take place from April 11 to April 14, 2024. This major golf event will be hosted at the renowned Augusta National Golf Club, located in Augusta, Georgia, United States. With its rich history and tradition, the Masters Tournament is eagerly anticipated by golf enthusiasts worldwide. Let's delve into the details of this esteemed event.


Augusta National Golf Club

The Augusta National Golf Club, the esteemed venue for the Masters Tournament, is an iconic golf course known for its pristine beauty and challenging layout. The club, located in Augusta, Georgia, has been the permanent home of the Masters since its inception. The course, spanning over 7,555 yards (6,908 meters) and with a par of 72, presents a rigorous yet captivating challenge for the world's top golfers.

Tournament Information

The 2024 Masters Tournament is part of the men's four major golf championships scheduled for the year. It holds significant importance in the golfing world, attracting top players from around the globe. The competition will feature a stellar lineup of participants, including those from the PGA Tour, European Tour, and Japan Golf Tour.

Notable Events and Participants

Several compelling storylines have emerged in the lead-up to the 2024 Masters Tournament. The event has seen the acceptance of special invitations by notable golfers such as Olesen, Hisatsune, and Niemann, adding an element of anticipation to the tournament. However, the absence of Bernhard Langer due to an Achilles injury has also captured attention.

Course Adjustments

In anticipation of the 2024 Masters Tournament, the Augusta National Golf Club has made strategic changes to the course. Notably, the par-5 second hole has been lengthened, adding an extra layer of challenge for the competitors. These alterations demonstrate the club's commitment to ensuring a fair yet demanding test for the participants.

With the world's top golfers vying for the coveted title, the tournament is set to captivate golf enthusiasts and spectators alike.
