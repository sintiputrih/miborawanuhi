Kirsten Dunst Films in a Bathing Suit 6 Months After Giving Birth

Publish date: 2024-03-23

Kirsten Dunst is back in action!

Six months after welcoming her first baby, son Ennis with fiancé Jesse Plemons, in May, the star filmed a scene in a bathing suit and a pair of jean shorts in Louisiana on November 19. The shoot was for her new Showtime series, On Becoming a God in Central Florida.

The actress debuted her slim post-baby body on the Emmys red carpet in September. She wore a floor length navy gown with a plunging neckline. Plemons, who wore a classic black tuxedo, was nominated for his role in Black Mirror’s “USS Callister” episode.
