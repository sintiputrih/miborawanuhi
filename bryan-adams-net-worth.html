Bryan Adams Career

Adams signed with A&M Records in 1978 and released his self-titled album, which had many chart-topping hits in Canada. He then released his sophomore album, ‘You Want It, You Got It,’ in 1981.

In 1983, he published his breakthrough album ‘Cuts Like a Knife.’ He released the album ‘Reckless,’ which debuted at number one on both the Canadian and US Billboard 200 charts.

Adams released his follow-up album, ‘Into the Fire,’ in 1987. His success extended into the 1990s, with the release of the platinum-certified ‘Waking Up the Neighbours’ album in 1991.

Along with his music, he has written and produced songs and albums for various other performers, including Rod Stewart, Sting, and Eric Clapton.
