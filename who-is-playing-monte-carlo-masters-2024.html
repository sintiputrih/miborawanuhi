Who is playing Monte Carlo Masters 2024?

Publish date: 2024-04-10

The Monte-Carlo Masters, also known as the Rolex Monte-Carlo Masters, is a prestigious ATP Masters 1000 tennis tournament held on outdoor clay courts.

The 2024 edition is the 117th of the annual Monte Carlo Masters tournament, sponsored by Rolex for the 15th time.

The tournament is set to take place from 7th to 14th April 2024 at the Monte Carlo Country Club in Roquebrune-Cap-Martin, France, although it is billed as Monte Carlo, Monaco.

Singles Players

The singles draw for the 2024 Monte-Carlo Masters is highlighted by the presence of some of the world's top-ranked and most promising tennis players. Here are some of the key players participating in the tournament:

Novak Djokovic (SRB) - Seed 1

Novak Djokovic, the world's top-ranked player as of 1st April 2024, is set to lead the pack at the 2024 Monte-Carlo Masters. The Serbian tennis maestro will be aiming to claim his third Monte-Carlo crown.

Jannik Sinner (ITA) - Seed 2

Jannik Sinner, the talented Italian player, holds the 2nd seed in the tournament and is expected to showcase his skills on the clay courts.

Carlos Alcaraz (ESP) - Seed 3

Spain's Carlos Alcaraz, the young and rising star in the tennis world, is another exciting talent to watch out for in the Monte-Carlo Masters.

Daniil Medvedev (RUS) - Seed 4

The Russian powerhouse, Daniil Medvedev, will be making his presence felt as the 4th seed in the tournament.

Alexander Zverev (GER) - Seed 5

Germany's Alexander Zverev, an established name in men's tennis, will also be competing in the prestigious tournament as the 5th seed.

Andrey Rublev (RUS) - Seed 6

Defending champion Andrey Rublev from Russia is back to defend his title at the 2024 Monte-Carlo Masters.

Other Notable Players

Other notable players to watch out for include Stefanos Tsitsipas (GRE), Holger Rune (DEN), and Rafael Nadal (ESP), who received entry using a protected ranking.

Doubles Teams

In addition to the singles draw, the doubles main draw at the 2024 Monte-Carlo Masters promises exciting action with the following teams set to compete:

Rohan Bopanna (IND) & Matthew Ebden (AUS) - Seed 1

The Indian-Australian duo of Rohan Bopanna and Matthew Ebden are the top seeds in the doubles draw.

Ivan Dodig (CRO) & Austin Krajicek (USA) - Seed 2

Croatia's Ivan Dodig and USA's Austin Krajicek are another formidable team as the 2nd seeds in the tournament.

Marcel Granollers (ESP) & Horacio Zeballos (ARG) - Seed 3

Spain's Marcel Granollers and Argentina's Horacio Zeballos are the 3rd seeds and are expected to put on a strong showing in the doubles event.

Rajeev Ram (USA) & Joe Salisbury (GBR) - Seed 4

The American-British pairing of Rajeev Ram and Joe Salisbury are another duo to keep an eye on in the doubles competition.

Wildcards and Qualifiers

In addition to the seeded players and teams, the tournament will also feature other entrants who received wildcards into the main draw and those who received entry via the qualifying draw. Marin Čilić and Tommy Paul are notable withdrawals, with Dominik Koepfer and Marcos Giron replacing them, respectively.

Prize Money and Points

The total financial commitment for the 2024 Rolex Monte-Carlo Masters is €6,410,670, with the prize money amounting to €5,950,575. The winner of the singles event is set to receive €919,075 and 1,000 points, while the doubles winners will claim €281,960 and 1,000 points.

The 2024 Monte-Carlo Masters is set to be an enthralling event with some of the biggest names in men's tennis vying for the prestigious title.

With top-seeded players like Novak Djokovic, Jannik Sinner, and defending champion Andrey Rublev, the tournament promises high-quality tennis action and intense competition on the picturesque clay courts of the Monte Carlo Country Club.

The presence of talented players and formidable doubles teams, combined with the allure of the Monte Carlo setting, ensures that the 2024 Monte-Carlo Masters will be a must-watch event for tennis enthusiasts around the world.

Overall, the tournament is expected to deliver thrilling matches, remarkable displays of skill, and captivating moments that highlight the enduring appeal of the Monte-Carlo Masters as a marquee event on the ATP Tour calendar.
