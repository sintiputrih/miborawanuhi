Compare Your Height to Sienna Guillory

Sienna Guillory is the daughter of US folk musician Isaac Guillory and English model Tina Thompson. Parents of Guillory encouraged her to portray herself artistically as she grew up and this culminated in her decision to become an actress. She had been educated at Gresham's Academy, Holt, Norfolk, England and performed in plays at night.Her acting break came when she was 16, and was cast in Riders (1993) television series. Guillory also took up modelling to promote her acting career, and participated in ads for such high-profile firms as Armani and Dolce & Gabbana, as well as gracing several magazine covers. More acting work in TV and films followed. Works include The Time Machine (2002), Love Actually (2003), and the tv series "Resident Evil."
