Miley Cyrus Released A New Song, Flowers

Miley Cyrus has released a new song, Flowers, which this writer believes is a female version of Bruno Mars' When I Was Your Man. No, the song is not an official remake, but it shares so many similarities. It seems to have the same rhythm, for one, it just goes faster. More so, the words. Let's take a look:

  • Bruno: I hope he buys you flowers / Miley: I can buy myself flowers
  • I hope he holds your hand / I can hold my own hand
  • Give you all his hours / Talk to myself for hours
  • Take you to every party, 'cause I remember how much you loved to dance / I can take myself dancing
  • Do all the things I should have done, when I was your man. / Yeah, I can love me better than you can

After further digging, it was found that Liam dedicated the Bruno song to her after their first breakup, and other fans were thinking the same thing.
