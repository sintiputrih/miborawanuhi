Who Is Diego Verdaguers Wife Amanda Miguel?

Publish date: 2024-06-05

Amanda Miguel, the wife of Diego Verdaguer, is an Argentine singer who has been active in the music scenes since 1980. Amanda Miguel and Diego Verdaguer walked down the aisle in 1975 and they have three children: María Gimena Boccadoro, Ana Victoria, and Ximena.

Amanda Miguel was born in Gaiman, Chubut, on June 1, 1956, hence, she is 65 years of age. Amanda Miguel has released a number of songs and some of them include Mi Buen Corazón, Castillos, Detrás De Mi Ventana, Ya Lo Sabia, Media Hora, and others.

Diego Verdaguer was a Mexican-Argentine singer and songwriter who was active in the media scenes for six decades. Diego Verdaguer was awarded by the Mexican Society of Authors and Composers in 2019.

Diego Verdaguer received multiple awards for his work and also ranked top on a number of platforms. Diego Verdaguer died on January 27, 2022, at the age of 70 years, as a result of complications from COVID-19.
